# Zypherpunk Hackathon - Grading Rubric & Scores

## Grading Criteria (1-10 scale)

### 1. Judge Appeal Score (1-10)
**Calculation**: Average appeal across all 10 judges, weighted by influence
- **High Weight**: cobie, balaji (viral/consumer influence)
- **Medium Weight**: eli, zooko, sean bowe, eddy (technical depth)
- **Medium Weight**: toly, mert (infrastructure)
- **Medium Weight**: illia (AI/UX)
- **Medium Weight**: imran khan (market fit)

**Scoring Guide**:
- 9-10: Appeals to 7+ judges strongly
- 7-8: Appeals to 5-6 judges strongly
- 5-6: Appeals to 3-4 judges
- 3-4: Appeals to 1-2 judges
- 1-2: Limited judge appeal

### 2. Technical Feasibility (1-10)
**Assessment**: Can this be built in hackathon timeframe?

**Scoring Guide**:
- 9-10: MVP achievable in 48-72 hours, well-defined tech stack
- 7-8: MVP achievable with some complexity, clear implementation path
- 5-6: Challenging but possible, requires significant expertise
- 3-4: Very difficult, may require cutting-edge research
- 1-2: Not feasible in hackathon timeframe

### 3. Virality Potential (1-10)
**Assessment**: Shareability, meme potential, network effects

**Scoring Guide**:
- 9-10: Highly shareable, memeable, strong network effects
- 7-8: Good shareability, some viral mechanics
- 5-6: Moderate shareability, limited viral potential
- 3-4: Low shareability, niche appeal
- 1-2: Not shareable, no viral potential

### 4. Uniqueness (1-10)
**Assessment**: Is this novel or does it exist already?

**Scoring Guide**:
- 9-10: Completely novel, first of its kind
- 7-8: Novel combination of existing tech, unique angle
- 5-6: Somewhat novel, improvements on existing
- 3-4: Similar to existing projects, incremental improvement
- 1-2: Generic, already exists

### 5. Prize Optimization (1-10)
**Assessment**: Prize potential across tracks and bounties

**Scoring Guide**:
- 9-10: $90k+ potential (multiple tracks + bounties)
- 7-8: $60-90k potential (track + bounty)
- 5-6: $40-60k potential (single track + bounty or multiple tracks)
- 3-4: $30-40k potential (single track)
- 1-2: <$30k potential
